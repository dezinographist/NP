Common questions

Everything you're probably wondering.

Is a heat pump really safer than an oil or propane heating system?
Yes, in three important ways. First, there's no combustion happening inside your home — no flame, no fuel storage tank, no risk of carbon monoxide leaks. Second, there's no flammable fuel being delivered or stored on your property. Third, you eliminate the exposure to volatile fuel pricing that's outside your control. A heat pump runs on electricity through a sealed, regulated system — the same kind of engineering used in your refrigerator, just scaled up.
Will a heat pump really work in a Central NY winter?
Yes. Modern Mitsubishi Electric heat pumps are engineered to deliver full heating capacity down to -13°F. We've installed them in homes from Skaneateles to Utica through some of the harshest CNY winters on record. Geothermal systems are even more reliable in cold weather because they pull heat from the consistent 50-55°F temperature of the ground beneath the frost line — your ground loop never feels winter.
How reliable are these systems long-term?
Extremely. The U.S. Department of Energy reports air-source heat pumps last 20–25 years versus 15–20 for gas furnaces and 10–15 for traditional AC units. Geothermal ground loops have a 50+ year lifespan according to IGSHPA (the International Ground Source Heat Pump Association). We back every install with our NP Shield™ 10+2 year extended labor warranty — the longest in the industry. If something needs service, we're the ones who installed it, and we're the ones who fix it.
What is NP Shield™ and how does it protect me?
NP Shield™ is our exclusive 10+2 year extended labor warranty available only to Central NY customers. It covers all eligible repairs with zero surprise bills. While many contractors offer 12-month labor warranties, we stand behind our engineering for 12 full years. That's our way of saying: we don't just install systems, we live with them — because we live in your community.
What happens if my power goes out — is my heating gone too?
Same as with any modern heating system (including oil and propane furnaces, which require electricity for their blowers and ignition). For homeowners who want full peace of mind, we install Generac whole-home backup generators that automatically start within seconds of an outage — keeping your heat pump, lights, refrigerator, and well pump running through any storm. Many CNY families pair their heat pump install with a Generac for complete resilience.
How much can I expect to save versus oil or propane?
The U.S. Department of Energy reports that homes switching from oil or propane to a high-efficiency heat pump typically save 40–65% annually on heating costs. Geothermal systems operate at 300–500% efficiency (COP), compared to 80–95% for natural gas furnaces and 100% for electric resistance heating. Your free in-home evaluation includes a personalized estimate for your specific home and fuel source.
How do NYS Clean Heat Rebates work — and will you help me apply?
NYS Clean Heat is a utility-funded rebate program (through NYSEG and other utilities) that reduces upfront installation costs for qualifying heat pump installations. Our team handles the entire application process on your behalf — we file the paperwork, you save the money. We were recognized as a 2024 Top-Performing Contractor by NYSEG & RG&E specifically for this work, so the rebates are in expert hands.
How long does installation take?
Most Air Source Heat Pump installations take 1–2 days. Geothermal installations vary based on loop type — horizontal loops typically complete in 1 week, vertical loops in 1–2 weeks, and pond loops depending on site access. Throughout the process, the same in-house team manages every step, so timelines stay predictable.
